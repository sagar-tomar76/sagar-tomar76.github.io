Item Durability Viewer

See the exact durability of any tool you are holding in Minecraft PE/BE using this addon.

HOW TO USE?
> Download the provided behavior pack.
> Import the behavior pack into Minecraft.
> Apply the behavior pack to your world.
> See "How to use?" section to use it properly.
> Hold a tool that has some durability.
> Sneak while holding the tool.
> Enjoy!
